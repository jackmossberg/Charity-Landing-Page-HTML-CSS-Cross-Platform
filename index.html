About Us

Hopscotch Charity is dedicated to improving the lives of children in need through various programs and initiatives.

For example. In 2017 we provided over $123,000 in scholarships to underprivileged children.

In 2019 we raised over $151,000 for our sister organization, which provided an estimated $500,000 in meals for the homeless.

And in 2020 we provided over $220,000 in bedding and clothing to children in need.

We are committed to making a positive impact in our community and beyond.
